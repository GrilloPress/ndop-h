Register your preference for sharing health data

Register your preferences

Use this service to manage your preferences for sharing your patient health information.

You must have an email address or phone number registered with an NHS service to continue online. Other ways to set your preferences instead.

Before you start

To continue you will need:

  • access to your email or mobile phone
  • your NHS number
Where can I find my NHS number?

You can find your NHS number on any document sent to you by the NHS. This may include:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • hospital referral letters
  • appointment letters
  • your NHS medical card

Ask your GP for help if you can't find your NHS number

Start

Verification steps

1

Enter your details

You enter your details so that we can look for your contact information in our patient database.

To confirm we have the correct contact details for you, you will need to provide your:

  • name
  • postcode
  • date of birth
  • NHS number

The information you provide will not be saved.

2

Choose your contact method

Using the details we hold in our database, we will send you a one-time verification code to your selected contact method.

This will be a mobile number or an email.

3

Enter your security code

Once you have chosen your contact method, you will be sent a code which will give you access to set your preference.

When you enter your code you will then be able to set your preference online.
