Design hypotheses

November

  1. If we summarise content a user needs to understand on the landing page, then users will understand the service better because they will be able to scan the homepage and capture the main features of the service
  2. If we display the summary content in a visually appealing way, then users will understand the service better because, as studies show, they will be spend more time engaging with the content directly related to it
